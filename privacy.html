RecipeSearch Privacy Policy

What Information do we store?

RecipeSearch collects and stores the following information about its users:
  • User Name
  • Email Address
  • Dietary Information
  • Food Preferences


Who can access this information?

Only Developers and Administrators of RecipeSearch can access stored information irrespective of any publicity settings. Stored information is only publicly visible if the user sets the information to public.

Information that RecipeSearch collects and/or stores are not sent to any third parties.

Removal of Information

Users can request removal of their information by deleting their account on our systems. This will delete any kind of Personally Identifiable Information and remove all reviews posted by this account.

Questions and Concerns

For any questions or concerns, please create an issue on the GitHub repository

This privacy policy is subject to change, any changes to this policy will be communicated on the GitHub repository
